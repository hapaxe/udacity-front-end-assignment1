Gas welding requires oxygen and a fuel gas (acetylene, MAPP, natural gas, propane).
Oxygen accelerates combustion, which can cause hazard if the oxygen content is above standard atmospheric percentage (20%).

If the hose of the oxyacetylene outfit, made of rubber and cotton, catches fire while under pressure, the fire cannot be extinguished using a fire extinguisher alone, because oxygen is being fed to the flames from inside the hose. First turn off the supply of oxygen and fuel gas.
